# APhA CPE and Certificate Programs

## How CPE Works
- All APhA CPE programs are ACPE-accredited
- Credits automatically report to CPE Monitor
- Pharmacists need 30 CPE hours per 2-year NABP e-Profile renewal cycle in most states
- APhA membership gives access to 300+ CPE hours — far more than most members need for renewal

## Featured Certificate Programs (Included with Membership)
- **Immunization Delivery**: 20 CPE hours — qualifies pharmacists to administer vaccines in most states
- **Diabetes Care**: 12 CPE hours — comprehensive diabetes management training
- **HIV Prevention**: 3-7 CPE hours — includes PrEP counseling
- **Travel Health**: 5 CPE hours — travel medicine and vaccinations
- **Motivational Interviewing**: 5 CPE hours — patient communication
- **MTM (Medication Therapy Management)**: 5 CPE hours
- **Naloxone and Opioid Safety**: 2 CPE hours
