# APhA Membership Benefits

## Continuing Pharmacy Education (CPE)
- Access to 300+ CPE hours through the APhA Learning Library
- Certificate programs: Immunization Delivery, Diabetes Care, HIV Prevention, Travel Health, Motivational Interviewing, MTM, Naloxone, and more
- All formats: self-paced online, live webinars, in-person workshops
- ACPE-accredited — all credits count toward state license renewal
- CPE Monitor integration for automatic credit reporting

## Journals and Publications
- **Pharmacy Today**: Monthly clinical and practice news
- **Journal of the American Pharmacists Association (JAPhA)**: Peer-reviewed research
- **Journal of Pharmaceutical Sciences (JPharmSci)**: For pharmaceutical scientists
- **Student Pharmacist**: For student members
- **PharmacyLibrary**: Digital access to APhA textbooks and references

## Events and Networking
- **APhA Annual Meeting & Exposition**: Largest US pharmacy conference (~10,000 attendees)
- **APhA Institute on Substance Use Disorders**
- **NP LIFE**: New Practitioner conference
- **Midyear Regional Meetings**: Regional networking events
- **APhA ENGAGE**: Online member community for peer discussion

## Career Services
- Job board with pharmacy-specific listings
- Career Pathways Program
- Residency and fellowship resources
- Resume and career coaching tools
- APhA ADVANCE mentorship platform

## Advocacy
- APhA represents pharmacists at the federal and state level
- APhA PAC (Political Action Committee)
- Advocacy Action Center — contact your legislators easily
- Input into national pharmacy guidelines and policies

## Tools and Resources
- Drug information tools and references
- Practice management resources
- Patient education materials
- APhA SmartBrief newsletter
