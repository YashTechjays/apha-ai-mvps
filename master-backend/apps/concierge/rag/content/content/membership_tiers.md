# APhA Membership Tiers

## Student Pharmacist Membership
- **Price**: ~$50/year (discounted heavily for students)
- **Who it's for**: Full-time pharmacy students enrolled in an accredited PharmD program
- **Key benefits**: APhA-ASP chapter membership, Student Pharmacist journal, discounted Annual Meeting registration, leadership development, access to the APhA Learning Library, networking with peers and mentors

## New Practitioner Membership
- **Price**: ~$115/year (first 3 years post-graduation)
- **Who it's for**: Pharmacists within their first 3 years after graduation
- **Key benefits**: All pharmacist benefits at reduced price, New Practitioner Network (NPN), NP LIFE event access, mentorship program, career development resources

## Pharmacist Membership (Full)
- **Price**: ~$195/year
- **Who it's for**: Licensed practicing pharmacists in all settings
- **Key benefits**: Full access to all CPE programs (300+ hours), JAPhA journal subscription, Pharmacy Today newsletter, Annual Meeting discounts, advocacy representation, APhA ENGAGE community, career services, PharmacyLibrary access

## Pharmacy Technician Membership
- **Price**: ~$75/year
- **Who it's for**: Practicing pharmacy technicians
- **Key benefits**: CPE credits, Pharmacy Today newsletter, community access, advocacy support, career resources

## Researcher / Pharmaceutical Scientist Membership
- **Price**: ~$195/year
- **Who it's for**: Pharmaceutical scientists, researchers, faculty
- **Key benefits**: Journal of Pharmaceutical Sciences (JPharmSci) access, research networking, publication discounts, Annual Meeting

## Supporter / Associate Membership
- **Price**: Varies
- **Who it's for**: Industry professionals, corporate partners, non-pharmacist healthcare professionals who support pharmacy

## Lifetime Membership
- **Price**: Lump sum (contact APhA for current rate)
- **Who it's for**: Long-term members who want permanent membership without annual renewals
